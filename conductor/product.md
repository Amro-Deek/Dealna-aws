# Product Guide: Dealna

## 1. Vision and Mission

**Vision:** To create the most trusted and secure campus-exclusive marketplace for university students, fostering a reliable community for trading and interaction.

**Mission:** To provide a secure, user-friendly mobile platform that connects students within their university for buying, selling, and exchanging goods. We aim to eliminate the risks and clutter of public platforms by ensuring every user is a verified member of the university community and by implementing robust security and fairness mechanisms.

## 2. Target Audience

*   **University Students:** The primary users of the platform. They are students enrolled in the university who need a safe and reliable way to buy, sell, or exchange items like textbooks, electronics, and daily necessities.
*   **Verified External Service Providers:** A secondary audience consisting of local businesses or individuals (e.g., print shops, housing providers) who are vetted and approved by the administration to offer their services to the student community in a controlled and trusted manner.

## 3. Goals & Features

### Goals
*   **Security:** Establish a trusted environment through mandatory university email verification and end-to-end encrypted communication.
*   **Fairness:** Implement a queue-based system for giveaways to ensure equal opportunities for all interested students.
*   **Trust:** Build user confidence through a transparent rating system and robust fraud detection mechanisms.
*   **Usability:** Provide an intuitive and easy-to-use interface for listing items, searching, and communicating.

### Key Features
*   **Secure User Verification:** All student users must register and verify their accounts using an official university email address.
*   **End-to-End Encrypted Chat:** A private and secure chat system protects all communication between users.
*   **Fair Giveaway Queue:** A unique queue-based system for "Giveaway" items ensures fairness by giving users a turn to claim the item.
*   **Role-Based Access Control:** Differentiates permissions between user types, including Students and Verified Providers.
*   **User Rating System:** Allows users to rate each other after a transaction is complete, building a community based on trust and reputation.
